Common Questions

Pranic Healing is an energy healing system that uses prana (life force) to accelerate the body's natural ability to heal. It works by cleansing and energizing the affected areas in the aura and chakras without physical touch.

Reiki is a Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that also promotes healing. A certified Master channels Universal Life Energy to the client, activating the body's natural healing processes and restoring physical and emotional well-being.

Javanese Magnetism is an ancient Indonesian energy practice that involves manipulating the body's magnetic field and energy flow. It is used to remove physical and emotional blockages, promoting deep relaxation and spiritual protection.

Distance healing is possible because energy is not limited by physical space. As a practitioner, I use intention and focused energy techniques to connect with your aura and transmit healing energy to you, wherever you are in the world.

Healing clears accumulated negative energy, stress, and trauma that lead to physical and emotional imbalance. It restores your energetic flow, allowing you to achieve balance, clarity, and access your full potential for a joyful life.

Anyone can benefit from healing, regardless of age or health status. It is beneficial for those seeking relief from stress, emotional blocks, physical ailments, and those wishing to improve their relationships, career, and spiritual growth.

In a session, you can expect to feel deeply relaxed, calm, and often a tingling or warmth as energy shifts. We will discuss your intentions beforehand, and afterward, I will provide guidance on what was addressed and how to maintain the positive results.
