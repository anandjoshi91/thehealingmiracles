About Reiki & Energy Healing

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique that promotes stress reduction, relaxation, and healing. The word "Reiki" comes from two Japanese words: "Rei" meaning "Universal" and "Ki" meaning "Life Force Energy." During a Reiki session, the practitioner channels this universal energy through their hands to the recipient, supporting the body's natural healing processes.

Reiki is non-invasive, gentle, and safe for people of all ages. It works on the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual levels to promote overall well-being.

Energy healing is based on the principle that everything is energy, including our bodies. When our energy becomes blocked, stagnant, or imbalanced, it can manifest as physical illness, emotional distress, or mental confusion.

Energy healers work to restore the natural flow of energy in the body by clearing blockages, balancing the chakras (energy centers), and channeling healing energy to areas that need it. This supports the body's innate ability to heal itself.

No, Reiki is not a religion. While it is spiritual in nature, it is not affiliated with any religious beliefs or practices. People of all faiths and belief systems can benefit from Reiki. It works with universal life force energy, which is recognized in various cultures and traditions around the world.

Chakras are energy centers located along the spine, from the base to the crown of the head. There are seven main chakras, each associated with different aspects of our physical, emotional, and spiritual health:

  • Root Chakra: Grounding, security, survival
  • Sacral Chakra: Creativity, emotions, sexuality
  • Solar Plexus: Personal power, confidence, will
  • Heart Chakra: Love, compassion, relationships
  • Throat Chakra: Communication, expression, truth
  • Third Eye: Intuition, wisdom, insight
  • Crown Chakra: Spirituality, connection to the divine

About Sessions

During a session, you will lie down or sit comfortably in a peaceful environment. I will place my hands on or near various parts of your body, channeling healing energy. Most people experience deep relaxation, warmth, tingling sensations, or a sense of peace.

Each person's experience is unique. Some people see colors, receive insights, or feel emotional releases. Others simply feel deeply relaxed. All experiences are valid and part of your healing journey.

Distance healing works on the principle that energy is not limited by physical space or time. Through focused intention and connection to universal energy, healing can be sent across any distance.

During a distance session, you will relax in your own space while I send healing energy to you. Many clients report feeling the same sensations they would during an in-person session—warmth, tingling, relaxation, and peace. The effectiveness is equal to in-person sessions.

Session lengths vary depending on the type of service:

  • Reiki Healing: 60-90 minutes
  • Chakra Balancing: 75-90 minutes
  • Distance Healing: 45-60 minutes
  • Mind Healing: 60-75 minutes
  • Energy Cleansing: 45-60 minutes

These times include a brief consultation before and integration time after the energy work.

The number of sessions varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some people experience significant improvement after just one session, while others benefit from a series of sessions for deeper healing.

For chronic conditions or deep-seated issues, I typically recommend starting with 3-5 sessions to establish a foundation. We will discuss your progress and adjust the plan as needed. Many people also choose to have regular maintenance sessions to support ongoing wellness.

Practical Information

To prepare for your session:

  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing
  • Avoid heavy meals immediately before the session
  • Stay hydrated
  • Set an intention for your healing
  • Come with an open mind and heart

For distance sessions, find a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed for the duration of the session.
