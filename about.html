My Healing Journey

Namaste! I am Parth Katira, a certified Reiki Master and energy healing practitioner. My journey into the world of healing began during a period of personal transformation when I discovered the profound impact that energy work can have on our physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Growing up, I always felt a deep connection to the subtle energies around us. This innate sensitivity led me to explore various healing modalities, eventually finding my true calling in Reiki and energy healing. After years of dedicated study under experienced masters and continuous personal practice, I have devoted my life to helping others experience the same transformative healing that changed my own life.

I believe that each person possesses an incredible capacity for self-healing. My role as a healer is to serve as a channel for universal life force energy, facilitating the natural healing process that exists within every individual. Through compassionate care and personalized attention, I work with each client to address their unique needs and support their journey toward wholeness.
